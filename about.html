About SKK

SKK Mobile Industry Trading, Inc. has been established to become a competitive mobile company in the country. It has been in the retail industry since 2006 starting out on SKK Mobile accessories specifically on chargers and batteries. It has established excellent legacy since then. In fact, we are one of the trusted brands of accessories in the Philippines and in Asia.

Our AIM is to reach out and provide quality and affordable mobile phones and accessories. We have particular range of products, from Dual SIM, QWERTY, Touch Screen and High-end Phones. Our MERCHANDISE is specially generated to indulge our client needs at very affordable prices.

On the first year of SKK Mobile, it became the fastest growing mobile phone company in the country. SKK has over 290 branches already, covering Luzon, Visayas, and Mindanao, in which are attributed by our hardworking dealers and partners.
